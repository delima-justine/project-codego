## 1. Getting Started

### System Requirements
*   **OS:** Android 7.0 (Nougat) or higher.
*   **Connectivity:** Internet connection (Wi-Fi or Mobile Data) is required for most features. GPS and Notification permissions are required for the Tracker and Emergency alerts.

### Installation
1.  Download and install the APK on your Android device.
2.  Launch the **ResQ PH** app from your app drawer.

### Onboarding & Authentication
*   **First Launch:** You will be greeted with an onboarding sequence explaining the app's core values. Click "Get Started" to proceed.
*   **Sign Up:** Create an account using your email, first name, last name, and password.
*   **Login:** Log in with your credentials. If your account was previously scheduled for deletion, you will be prompted to reactivate it.
*   **Emergency Access:** You can access the **Emergency Hotlines** tab immediately without logging in via the "EMERGENCY CONTACTS" button on the login screen.

## 3. Feature Guide

### 3.1 Sharing Hub (Home)
The **Sharing Hub** is where the community connects through survival stories and disaster advice.

*   **Browsing Posts:** 
    *   **Filtering:** Use the category row at the top (e.g., "Survival Story", "Disaster Tip", "Advice") to filter content.
    *   **Pagination:** Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons at the bottom of the feed to navigate through pages of posts.
*   **Interactions:**
    *   **Like:** Tap the heart icon to show support for a post.
    *   **Comment:** Tap the message icon to view or add comments. You can edit or delete your own comments via the three-dot menu next to them.
*   **Creating a Post:**
    1.  Tap the **"Share Your Experience"** button.
    2.  Select a category and write your content.
    3.  Tap "Post" to share.
*   **Managing Posts:** Tap the **three-dot menu** on your own posts to **Edit** or **Delete** them.

### 3.2 Emergency Hotlines
A comprehensive directory designed for quick access, even offline.

*   **Search:** Use the search bar to quickly find specific emergency contacts or agencies.
*   **Direct Calling:** Tap the **call button (üìû)** on any contact card to instantly open your phone's dialer app with the emergency number pre-filled. Simply press call in your phone app to complete the connection.
*   **Pagination:** Browse through emergency contacts efficiently with 3 contacts displayed per page. Use the page number buttons or "Previous"/"Next" buttons at the bottom to navigate.
*   **Offline Access:** All emergency contacts are stored locally and remain fully accessible without an internet connection.

### 3.3 News & Updates
Stay informed with curated disaster-related news.

*   **Visual Feed:** News cards include headlines, summaries, and images when available.
*   **Real-time Updates:** The feed fetches the latest news automatically when you open the tab.

### 3.4 Live Tracker & Emergency Broadcast
Visualize the community and broadcast your status during crises.

*   **Permissions:** Requires **Location** (Fine) and **Notification** permissions.
*   **Sharing Location:** Toggle the "Sharing Location" switch.
    *   **ON (Green):** You appear as a blue marker ("Me") to others.
    *   **OFF (Gray):** Your location is hidden and removed from the server.
*   **üö® Emergency Mode:**
    *   Tap the **"EMERGENCY!"** button to start broadcasting.
    *   Your marker turns **RED** and displays "üö® NEED HELP! üö®".
    *   **Proximity Alerts:** Other users within a **5km radius** will receive a push notification alerting them of your emergency.
    *   While in Emergency Mode, location sharing cannot be disabled until you tap "CANCEL EMERGENCY".
*   **Active Users List:** View a list of nearby active users and their status directly on the map interface.

### 3.5 Profile Management & Security
*   **Avatars:** Your profile features a colorful avatar generated based on your initials.
*   **Edit Profile:** Update your first name, last name, or email address.
*   **Logout:** Securely sign out from the Profile screen.
*   **Account Deletion & Reactivation:**
    *   **Soft Delete:** Initiating deletion hides your data immediately and schedules permanent removal after **30 days**.
    *   **Reactivation:** To cancel deletion, simply log back in within the 30-day window and select **"Reactivate Account"** when prompted.

### 3.6 About ResQ PH
*   Learn about our mission to bridge the gap between emergency services and Filipinos.
*   Access the digital manual and version information.

## 4. Offline Mode

ResQ PH ensures basic safety features are available without internet.

*   **Works Offline:**
    *   **Emergency Hotlines:** The directory is fully accessible. Search and filtering work on local data.
*   **Requires Internet:**
    *   **Sharing Hub:** Loading, liking, commenting, and posting.
    *   **News:** Fetching latest articles.
    *   **Live Tracker:** Map tiles, location sharing, and receiving emergency alerts.

## 5. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Tab is grayed out** | Ensure you have an active internet connection and are logged in. The Emergency tab is the only one available offline/guest. |
| **"EMERGENCY!" button not working** | Check if Location permissions are granted. Emergency mode requires an active GPS signal to broadcast. |
| **Not receiving alerts** | Ensure **Notification permissions** are enabled in your device settings for ResQ PH. |
| **Map is blank** | Map tiles require internet to download. If you are online but it's blank, try zooming in/out to refresh. |
| **Login fails after deletion request** | If the 30-day grace period has passed, your account is permanently removed and cannot be recovered. |
