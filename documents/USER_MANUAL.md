## 1. Getting Started

### System Requirements
*   **OS:** Android 7.0 (Nougat) or higher.
*   **Connectivity:** Internet connection (Wi-Fi or Mobile Data) is required for most features. GPS is required for the Tracker.

### Installation
1.  Download and install the APK on your Android device.
2.  Launch the **ResQ PH** app from your app drawer.

### Onboarding & Authentication
*   **First Launch:** You will be greeted with an onboarding sequence explaining the app's core values. Click "Get Started" to proceed.
*   **Sign Up:** If you are a new user, create an account using your email and password.
*   **Login:** Existing users can log in with their credentials.
*   **Emergency Access:** You can access the **Emergency Hotlines** tab immediately without logging in if you are in a hurry, though other features will be locked.

## 3. Feature Guide

### 3.1 Sharing Hub (Home)
The **Sharing Hub** is where the community connects. Here you can read survival stories, tips, and general advice.

*   **Viewing Posts:** Scroll through the feed to read posts from other users.
*   **Filtering:** Use the category buttons at the top (e.g., "Survival Story", "Disaster Tip") to see specific types of content.
*   **Creating a Post:**
    1.  Tap the **"Share Your Experience"** button.
    2.  Select a category.
    3.  Write your content.
    4.  Tap "Post" to share it with the community.
*   **Managing Posts:**
    *   Tap the **three-dot menu** on your own posts to **Edit** or **Delete** them.

### 3.2 Emergency Hotlines
This is the most critical feature of the app, designed for quick access.

*   **Direct Calling:** Tap on any contact card (e.g., "National Emergency Hotline 911") to open your phone's dialer with the number pre-filled.
*   **Categories:** Contacts are organized by type (Police, Fire, Medical, Rescue) for faster searching.

### 3.3 News & Updates
Stay informed about the current situation.

*   **Latest News:** The screen automatically fetches the latest disaster-related news and weather updates affecting the Philippines.
*   **Details:** Tap on a news card to read the summary and source.

### 3.4 Live Tracker
Visualize the community on a map.

*   **Permissions:** Upon first accessing this tab, you will be asked to grant **Location Permissions**. This is required for the feature to work.
*   **Sharing Location:** Toggle the switch at the bottom to **"Sharing Location"**.
    *   **ON:** You are visible to others on the map as a blue marker ("Me"). Your location updates in real-time.
    *   **OFF:** You are invisible to others, and your location is not uploaded.
*   **Viewing Others:** You can see other active users as colored dots on the map. Tap a dot to see their username.

### 3.5 Profile Management & Security
*   **Edit Profile:** Go to the **Profile** tab (or "Account" in the menu) to update your display name or email.
*   **Logout:** Scroll to the bottom of the Profile screen to log out of your account.
*   **Delete Account (Soft Delete):**
    *   Located at the bottom of the Profile screen in **soft red**.
    *   When initiated, your account is scheduled for permanent deletion after **30 days**.
    *   Your data is hidden from the community immediately.
*   **Account Reactivation:**
    *   If you change your mind within the 30-day grace period, simply log in to your account.
    *   A prompt will appear asking if you want to **Reactivate Account**.
    *   Choosing "Reactivate" will restore your account fully and cancel the deletion schedule.

## 4. Offline Mode

ResQ PH is designed to be useful even when connectivity fails.

*   **Works Offline:**
    *   **Emergency Hotlines:** The directory is stored locally on your device. You can access numbers and call them (provided you have cellular service) even without the internet.
*   **Requires Internet:**
    *   Sharing Hub (Loading and posting stories).
    *   News feed updates.
    *   Live Tracker (Map tiles and user locations).

## 5. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **"No Internet Connection"** | Check your Wi-Fi or Mobile Data settings. The Home, News, and Tracker tabs require an active connection. |
| **Location not updating** | Ensure GPS is enabled on your device and you have granted "Fine Location" permission to the app. |
| **Map is blank** | The map requires internet to load the visual tiles. Ensure you are online. |
| **Cannot Login** | Verify your email and password. If your account was scheduled for deletion, you can reactivate it during login. |
| **Reactivation Prompt Not Appearing** | Ensure you are logging in with the correct credentials. If the 30-day period has passed, the account is permanently removed. |
